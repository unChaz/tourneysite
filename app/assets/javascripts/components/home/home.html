About Ting Mobile

Ting is the smarter choice for cell phone service.

Ting does rates, not plans. How you use your phone determines what you pay. Use less? Pay less. Every month is different.

Ting has two nationwide LTE network offerings, GSM (T-Mobile) and CDMA (Sprint), so you can choose the network that works best in your area.

The more often you're around Wi-Fi, the cheaper your phone bill will be.
